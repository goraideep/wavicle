About SignSpeak

SignSpeak is an AI-powered platform designed to bridge the communication gap between the deaf and hearing communities. It recognizes sign language gestures in real time and translates spoken/text input into animated signs.

📱 Multi-Platform

Works seamlessly across web and mobile devices.

🤖 AI & ML Integration

Powered by MediaPipe & AI models for high accuracy.

🌍 Inclusivity

Supports multiple sign languages to connect communities worldwide.
